# Features

## What It Does

analyze.me connects to your Google Docs and uses advanced AI to help you understand your personal documents better. Get instant insights about your thoughts, goals, and emotions with just a few clicks.

## Key Features

- **Connect Your Google Docs** - Seamlessly access and select any document from your Google Drive
- **5 Ready-to-Use Analysis Types** - Analyze key themes, mood & sentiment, goals & progress, strengths & weaknesses, or get self-improvement suggestions
- **Choose Your Detail Level** - Get quick summaries, balanced insights, or comprehensive deep-dives with adjustable response lengths
- **Save & Export** - Download your analysis as PDF or copy to clipboard for easy sharing
- **Track Your History** - Keep a record of all your past analyses to see how your thoughts evolve over time
- **Stunning Cyberpunk Interface** - Enjoy a modern, futuristic design with neon accents and smooth animations
